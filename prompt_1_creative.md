# Experiment: Creativity vs Constraint Balance

## Prompt
Write a short horror story in exactly 50 words.

## AI Response
The house whispered at night. Doors creaked open without touch. Sarah counted her breaths—one, two, three—but the mirror showed four. She turned. Nothing. The whisper grew louder, calling her name. Morning came. The mirror was empty. Sarah never existed, yet the house still whispered her story.

## Prompt Intent
To test whether the model can follow strict constraints (word count) while still maintaining creative quality.

## Observations
- Strong narrative tone and atmosphere  
- Slightly exceeds the word limit  
- Prioritizes storytelling over precision  

## Issue
- Weak enforcement of strict constraints  
- Focus shifts toward creativity instead of accuracy  

## Insight
When given creative tasks with strict limits, the model tends to relax constraints in favor of better storytelling, suggesting a trade-off between precision and output quality.
