# Prompt Experiment 1: Creative Writing

## Prompt
Write a short horror story in exactly 50 words.

## AI Response
The house whispered at night. Doors creaked open without touch. Sarah counted her breaths—one, two, three—but the mirror showed four. She turned. Nothing. The whisper grew louder, calling her name. Morning came. The mirror was empty. Sarah never existed, yet the house still whispered her story.

## Rating
Good

## Issues
- Slight deviation from exact 50-word constraint  
- Minor repetition in phrasing  

## Why It's Problematic
- Failing strict constraints reduces instruction-following accuracy  
- Repetition slightly weakens writing quality  

## Improved Response
The house whispered at night. Doors creaked open without touch. Sarah counted her breaths—one, two, three—but the mirror showed four. She turned. Nothing. The whisper called her name again. Morning came, but Sarah was gone. Only the house remembered her.
